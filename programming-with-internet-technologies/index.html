Robert L. Lovelace

My hobbies include:

  • Bowling I bowl in three different leagues throughout the week. My average is 157.
  • Cooking My favorite styles are Mediterranean, Southeast Asian, and Southern Country dishes.
  • Traveling I have traveled to 13 countries and 43 states.

I served two tours aboard the U.S.S. John C. Stennis (CVN-74) aircraft carrier.

My first cruise began in January 1998 from Norfolk, VA to San Diego in August 1998. We traveled through the Strait of Gibraltar, Suez Canal, and Strait of Hormuz to enter the Persian Gulf. We visited UAE (Jebel Ali, Dubai), Bahrain, then continued on to Fremantle and Perth, Australia, and Hobart, Tasmania before arriving in San Diego via Hawai‘i.

My second cruise (WestPac) was from Feb–Aug 2000, visiting many of the same locations plus Malaysia, Hong Kong, and South Korea.

Between deployments, our ship visited Nova Scotia, Saint Martin, and Vancouver, BC during work-ups.

I served as a Quartermaster (QM3 SW/AW). I earned designations as an Enlisted Surface Warfare Specialist (ESWS) and Enlisted Aviation Warfare Specialist (EAWS).
